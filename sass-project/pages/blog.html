Mexico City - Destinations

Discover destinations within Mexico City! Together we will travel through the city and I will be your tour guide while taking a journey through some of the most iconic and historic areas of the city that make it an amazing place to visit.

Mexico City - Outskirts

Together we discover the city limits and outskirts of Mexico City. There are unique and amazing places that are just outside of the inner city that offer culture with a different taste of exploration.

Every culture has its own unique food that defines it's origins. Come with me as we explore the food of Mexico City! We will explore restaurants, street vendors, and even bars while trying familiar and not-so-familiar foods and drinks.
